Dashboard

1. Identifying and Solving Problems

Approach: Use methods like Root Cause Analysis or Fishbone Diagram to analyze company processes, identify key problems, and understand their causes.

Types of Analysis:

  • Descriptive Analysis – Provides information about existing processes.
  • Process Mining – Identifies problems in processes.

Technologies:

  • Power BI, Tableau – For data visualization and analysis.
  • Python (pandas, NumPy) – For data analysis.
  • JIRA, Asana – For issue management and tracking.

2. Data-Driven Decision Making

Approach: Base decisions on data using the Data-Driven Decision Making (DDDM) principle. Collect, analyze, and forecast data.

Types of Analysis:

  • Diagnostic Analysis – Identifies why a problem occurred.
  • Predictive Analysis – Forecasts future trends.

Technologies:

  • SQL – For database management and analysis.
  • Python (scikit-learn) or R – For predictive models.
  • Power BI, Looker – For decision visualization.

3. Process Optimization

Approach: Use Lean Six Sigma or BPM (Business Process Management) methodologies to optimize company production, delivery, and other processes.

Types of Analysis:

  • Process Analysis – Evaluates and optimizes process steps.
  • Efficiency Analysis – Identifies efficiency.

Technologies:

  • Bizagi, Lucidchart – For process mapping.
  • Python (SimPy) – For process simulation.
  • ERP systems (SAP, Odoo) – For resource management.

4. Enhancing Competitiveness

Approach: Use Competitive Analysis and SWOT analysis to analyze the market and competitors.

Types of Analysis:

  • Benchmarking – Compare your company with competitors.
  • Market Analysis – Identify market share and growth opportunities.

Technologies:

  • Google Trends, SEMrush – For market analysis.
  • Power BI, Excel – For comparison and data analysis.
  • Web scraping (Python – BeautifulSoup, Selenium) – For gathering data from competitors.

5. Economic Efficiency and Growth

Approach: Use Financial Analysis and ROI (Return on Investment) calculations to analyze the company's financial performance and identify new revenue sources.

Types of Analysis:

  • Financial Analysis – Evaluate income, expenses, and profit.
  • Growth Analysis – Study company growth trends.

Technologies:

  • Excel, Google Sheets – For financial calculations.
  • Power BI, QuickBooks – For monitoring financial metrics.
  • Python (matplotlib, pandas) – For financial data analysis.

6. Understanding Customer Needs

Approach: Use Customer Segmentation and Sentiment Analysis methods to understand customer behavior and needs.

Types of Analysis:

  • Customer Analysis – Segment customer data.
  • Behavioral Analysis – Analyze customer purchasing behavior.

Technologies:

  • Python (NLTK, TextBlob) – For analyzing customer feedback.
  • Google Analytics – For website traffic analysis.
  • CRM systems (HubSpot, Zoho) – For managing customer data.

7. Reducing Risks

Approach: Use Risk Assessment and Scenario Analysis to manage risks and develop countermeasures.

Types of Analysis:

  • Risk Analysis – Identify and evaluate potential risks.
  • Scenario Analysis – Analyze the impact of risks in different scenarios.

Technologies:

  • Monte Carlo Simulation (Python – numpy, scipy) – For probability analysis.
  • Power BI, Excel – For visualizing risk data.
  • GRC systems (Governance, Risk, Compliance) – For managing risks.
